Open-mode




Configurable links

Here you can configure the links to be generated.

  • 'name' (second column) is what will appear in the menu.
  • 'value' (third column) is the link generated for this menu-entry.
  • The checkboxes allow to enable/disable the entries in the menu.

Following substitutions are made and give the flexibility:

  • $msgid$ => the 'message-id' such as in the header of the mail.
  • $cblink$ => the identifier generated for a cbthunderlink.
  • $author$, $author_name$, $author_email$ => the author of the mail, or only their name / email address.
  • $date_iso$, $time_iso$, $date_locale$, $time_locale$ => the UTC date / time of the mail, formatted either as ISO or according to your locale settings.
  • $subject$, $subject_filtered => the mail's subject (the filtered subject replaces a few characters that frequently cause trouble, with a focus on org-mode).

Typical default entries are:

  • cbthunderlink : cbthunderlink://$cblink$
  • thunderlink : thunderlink://messageid=$msgid$
